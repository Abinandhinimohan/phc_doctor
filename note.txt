 DDHS (Deputy Director of Health Services)
Full Form: Deputy Director of Health Services
🔹 DDHS is a senior government health official responsible for overseeing multiple PHCs in a district or region.
🔹 Reports to the State Health Directorate and implements government health programs.


HMS (Hospital Management System)
Full Form: Hospital Management System
🔹 HMS is a software system used in PHCs, hospitals, and healthcare facilities for managing patient records, doctor schedules, attendance, and billing.
